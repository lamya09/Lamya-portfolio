Who I am

About Me

Hey! I'm Lamya, a Computer Science Engineering student at Manipal University Jaipur with a genuine curiosity for learning and experimenting. Born and raised in Pune, I developed an early interest in technology right at home before moving to Jaipur for my engineering degree.

My formal journey started at VIBGYOR High, where I chose the Science stream with Computer Science. That foundation began with Java in 10th grade, moved into Python and SQL during 11th and 12th, and has now grown into hands-on web development as part of the junior web development committee at MUJ ACM SIGBed.

What excites me most about technology is the balance between logic and creativity, and how they come together to build solutions that create real impact. I believe in learning by building, growing through collaboration, and using every tool available to turn ideas into meaningful solutions.
